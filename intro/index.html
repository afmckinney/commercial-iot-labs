Introduction to the Intel® IoT Gateway Product Line and Developer Program

Introduction & Overview

Objective

Get off to a good start by making sure your computer, Intel Edison, and Intel IoT Gateway are ready for IoT development.

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Connect to your Intel IoT Gateway using SSH in order to run commands on the gateway.
  • Connect to your Intel Edison using a serial connection in order to configure the board.
  • Use the Intel XDK to deploy an application to your Intel Edison.
  • Blink the onboard LED of your Intel Edison.

Step 1: Login to your Intel Iot Gateway

Establish an SSH connection to your Intel® IoT Gateway using PuTTY installed on your development computer.

Go to Step 1 »

Step 2: Set up your Intel Edison

Connect your development computer to the Intel Edison using the supplied micro-USB cables.

Go to Step 2 »

Step 3: Login to your Intel Edison

Establish a serial connection to your IoT board using PuTTY installed on your development computer. Use Windows Device Manager to find the COM port number for the "USB Serial Port" connection.

Go to Step 3 »

Step 4: Deploy a sample application with the Intel® XDK IoT Edition

Create and run a Hello Word project (blinking the onboard LED) on your Intel® IoT board based on the "Onboard LED Blink" starter template.

Go to Step 4 »
